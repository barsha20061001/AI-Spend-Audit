# METRICS

## Core Product Metrics

### 1. Audit Completion Rate
Percentage of users who start the audit and successfully generate results.

Why it matters:
Measures onboarding clarity and product usability.

---

### 2. Lead Conversion Rate
Percentage of users who submit their contact information after seeing audit results.

Why it matters:
Primary indicator of business value and intent quality.

---

### 3. Average Estimated Monthly Savings
Average savings identified per audit session.

Why it matters:
Helps measure perceived product usefulness and value proposition strength.

---

### 4. High-Savings Audit Percentage
Percentage of audits identifying savings above a defined threshold (e.g. $500/month).

Why it matters:
Indicates how often Credex-style consultation opportunities appear.

---

### 5. Share Rate
Percentage of generated audits shared through copy link or social sharing actions.

Why it matters:
Measures virality and product-led distribution potential.

---

## Engineering Metrics

### 6. Test Pass Rate
Percentage of automated tests passing in CI.

Target:
100%

---

### 7. CI Reliability
Percentage of successful GitHub Actions runs.

Why it matters:
Ensures deployment and testing stability.

---

### 8. Error-Free Audit Sessions
Percentage of sessions completed without frontend or backend failures.

Why it matters:
Measures production readiness and UX reliability.
