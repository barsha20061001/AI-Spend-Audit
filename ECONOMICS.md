## Business Opportunity

If the platform identifies large savings opportunities, teams may:
- request deeper audits
- seek procurement help
- centralize AI vendor management
- optimize infrastructure usage

---

## Potential Revenue Streams

### 1. SaaS Subscription
Monthly fee for ongoing spend monitoring.

### 2. Consulting
Custom optimization recommendations for high-spend teams.

### 3. Enterprise Dashboard
Centralized AI governance and usage visibility.

### 4. Vendor Partnerships
Referral or procurement partnerships with AI tooling providers.

---

## Risks

- AI pricing changes rapidly
- Recommendations may become outdated
- Enterprise buyers may require stronger financial accuracy
- API usage optimization can vary significantly across teams

---

## Long-Term Potential

As AI adoption grows, operational visibility and cost optimization may become a dedicated category similar to cloud cost management platforms.
