## FAQ

### 1. Is this free?
Yes. The audit is free and does not require signup before showing results.

### 2. What tools does it support?
It supports Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, OpenAI API, Anthropic API, Gemini, and Windsurf.

### 3. Do I need to connect billing accounts?
No. Users manually enter their current tools, plans, seats, and monthly spend.

### 4. How are savings calculated?
Savings are calculated through deterministic audit rules based on plan pricing, team size, usage fit, and cheaper available alternatives.

### 5. When does Credex appear?
Credex is surfaced when the audit shows meaningful savings potential, especially for teams with high monthly AI spend.
